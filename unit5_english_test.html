📖 Section 5 – True or False (Grammar Rules)
Question 21
We use "was" with the subjects: I, he, she, it.
✅ PRAWDA — I was / he was / she was / it was. To są wszystko formy w liczbie pojedynczej → używamy was.
Question 22
The negative of "was" is "weren't".
✅ FAŁSZ — Przeczenie od was to wasn't (was not).   weren't = were not (przeczenie w liczbie mnogiej).
Question 23
"There were some buses." → The negative form is: "There weren't any buses."
✅ PRAWDA — Twierdzenie (liczba mnoga): There were some… → Przeczenie: There weren't any…
Question 24
To ask a question about "you" in the past, we say: "Was you at the park?"
✅ FAŁSZ — Poprawna forma to "Were you at the park?" — z "you" zawsze używamy were, nigdy was.
Question 25
"Was there a cinema?" asks about ONE cinema (singular). "Were there any cinemas?" asks about MORE THAN ONE (plural).
✅ PRAWDA — Was there a…? = liczba pojedyncza  |  Were there any…? = liczba mnoga. Ta sama zasada: there was a… / there were some…
